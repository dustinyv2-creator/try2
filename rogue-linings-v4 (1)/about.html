Ultimate Linings Training & Partnership

Ultimate Linings offers comprehensive polyurea factory training programs that include hands‑on experience, best practices, advanced techniques, and troubleshooting guidance. Training can be delivered at their state‑of‑the‑art spray lab or on‑site at your facility, tailored to specific needs and built to ensure confidence in applying their coatings. The goal is to help contractors and applicators advance their skills and grow their business.

  • Hands‑on learning: Real‑world polyurea application with guidance from experienced professionals.
  • Expert guidance: A team with 200+ years of combined experience in polyurea and equipment.
  • Customization: Programs tailored for newcomers or pros optimizing their process.
  • Equipment & maintenance: Help with equipment selection, setup, and maintenance.
  • On‑site support: Training available at your facility, with ongoing technical support.
  • Certification: Earn certification to stand out in the industry.
